Famous Temples

🛕
📍 Thanjavur
Thanjavur
Brihadeeswarar Temple

UNESCO World Heritage Site. The grand Chola masterpiece dedicated to Lord Shiva, built by Raja Raja Chola I over 1000 years ago.

Near Thanjavur
Gangaikondacholapuram

A living testimony to the glory of the Chola Empire. Another UNESCO gem standing majestically in rural Tamil Nadu.

Darasuram
Airavatesvara Temple

The third Great Living Chola Temple, renowned for exquisite stone carvings and artistic Dravidian architecture.

📍 Kumbakonam
Navagraha Temples
Suriyanar Temple

Famous Navagraha temple near Kumbakonam dedicated to the Sun God (Surya). doshas.

Kumbakonam
Sarangapani Temple

The largest Vishnu temple in Kumbakonam with a magnificent 11-storey gopuram.

Kumbakonam
Mahamaham Tank

The sacred tank where the holy waters of all rivers converge every 12 years during Mahamaham festival.

Kumbakonam
Kumbeshwarar Temple

The presiding deity temple of Kumbakonam, dedicated to Lord Shiva, with an ancient nine-storey Rajagopuram.
